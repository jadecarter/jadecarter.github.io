It's hot in the South and Kayla Steen, Atlanta born and raised, matches that heat with in record and on stage. Vocally, she's a vinyl lovers dream -her lyrics floating out of speakers, holding you tight and making you hit repeat again and again.

On stage you can't take your eyes off her, inspired by of the storytelling of Stevie Nicks, Rihanna's cool, the allure of Janet and the vocal ability of Beyonce - you'll lose your mind, bewitched on the dance floor as she joyfully takes you along for the ride.

This songbird has graced the stage as an opening-act for top-charting artists such as Kendrick Lamar and Melanie Fiona. Taking cues from fellow triple threats James Fauntleroy and Brittany Bosco, she's a singer songwriter and producer quickly moving from underground favorite to mainstream success.

If her music doesn't kill you, her smile will - get to know her now so you can look back in 20 years and say "Damn, can't believe she's still getting better.".
